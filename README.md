## Why Mixero Stands Out

Unlike traditional **btc mixers** that simply pool Bitcoin together, Mixero utilizes a unique Monero (XMR) bridge. By routing your BTC through the most privacy-centric blockchain in existence before returning it to you, Mixero ensures that even the most advanced blockchain analysis tools cannot trace the origin of your funds.

## Key Features

- **XMR Bridge Technology** — Converts BTC to Monero, mixes it natively on the private XMR ledger, and converts it back to BTC, completely breaking the on-chain link.
- **Multi-Coin Support** — While primarily known as a top-tier **crypto mixer** for Bitcoin, Mixero supports over 12 different cryptocurrencies, making it a versatile privacy tool.
- **CoinJoin Protocol** — For users who prefer to stay entirely within the Bitcoin ecosystem, Mixero also offers standard CoinJoin mixing with a massive liquidity pool.
- **No-Log Policy** — All connection logs and order details are purged from servers immediately after the transaction is finalized.
- **Tor Integration** — The platform is fully accessible via the Tor network, ensuring your IP address remains hidden while you mix.
- **Multiple Output Addresses** — Distribute your clean coins across up to 5 different wallet addresses for layered anonymity.
